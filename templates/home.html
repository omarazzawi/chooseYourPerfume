How to Pick the Perfect Perfume

With endless perfumes to explore, I've spent years reading, testing, and falling in love with men's fragrances. Through this journey, I've learned one golden rule: Before choosing your scent, always ask yourself these key questions:

  • What is the occasion?
    • Romantic date? Family gathering? Professional meeting?
  • Where will you be?
    • Office formal? Nightclub bold? Casual daytime?
  • When will you wear it?
    • Summer freshness? Winter warmth? Daytime light or nighttime intense?

Overwhelmed by those questions? Keep it simple—there are 3 foolproof scents that always work:
